Depression Salad

Description

A hearty and wholesome salad for those really good days.

Ingredients:

  • 2 bananas
  • Peanut butter
  • Star-shaped sprinkles

Steps:

  1. Be depressed and lethargic.
  2. Be deadly allergic to peanuts.
  3. Unpeel bananas
  4. Gently place bananas on plate.
  5. Smear peanut butter on both bananas.
  6. Add star-shaped sprinkles for aestethic.
  7. Enjoy your depression salad.
