Lasagna

Description

This is how you make pre-made lasagna - a staple meal for the energetically deprived.

Ingredients:

  • Store-bought frozen lasagna
  • Microwave
  • A cold glass of tap water

Steps:

  1. Open package.
  2. Remove plastic from inner-packaging.
  3. Place lasagna in microwave at highest heat setting for about 3,5 minutes.
  4. Let lasagna cool off for a minute or so before eating.
  5. Enjoy with a cool glass of tap water.
