A Glass of Milk

Description

How to pour a glass of milk.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • A glass

Steps:

  1. Remove milk from refrigerator.
  2. Close refrigerator door.
  3. Unscrew milk package.
  4. Remove your glass of choice from your cupboards.
  5. Pour milk from container into glass.
  6. Drink your milk.
  7. Have some more.
